Frequently Asked Questions

What is an eSIM?

An eSIM is a digital SIM card built into your phone. It lets you activate a cellular plan without inserting a physical SIM card.

Is my phone compatible?

Most recent Android and iPhones support eSIM. Check your settings or manufacturer's website.

How do I install the eSIM?

After sign-up, we send you a QR code. Simply scan it with your phone's camera and follow the setup instructions.
