Frequently Asked Questions

What is an eSIM?

An eSIM is a digital SIM that allows you to activate a cellular plan without a physical SIM card.

Is my phone compatible?

Most modern smartphones support eSIM. Check your device settings or manufacturer's website for compatibility.

How do I install the eSIM?

After signing up, you'll receive a QR code. Scan it with your phone to install the eSIM profile.
